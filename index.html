30–60 seconds each. A little discomfort is normal. Rate what you find — 1 is nothing, 5 is very tender.
Optional — Layer 3
One more optional layer.
Two short movement videos — an overhead squat and a push-up — give us the clearest picture of how you move. Set aside about 5 minutes and floor space. You'll need your phone on a surface.
Layer 3 — Movement Video
Two movements.
Don't try to perform perfectly — we want your natural pattern.
Movement 1 — Overhead Squat
1
Place your phone at hip height from the front. Full body visible, head to toe.
2
Stand shoulder-width, toes slightly out. Raise both arms straight overhead, hands wider than shoulders.
3
Keep arms up the entire time. Lower into a squat as deep as comfortable, then back up. 5 reps slowly.
4
Side view too if you can — helpful but not required.
